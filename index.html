SA LOOB NG PAGLABAN
A fairer, healthier, and better understanding of addiction is within reach.
But only if we are willing to know and empathize of their struggles
Discovering addiction's complexities is like exploring different landscapes in our minds. It's a deviation influenced by diverse factors. From stress storms to learned scripts, this narrative experience unveils how individuals cope and navigate towards resilience and recovery.
THE STIGMA AROUND ADDICTION DOES NOT MAKE OUR COMMUNITIES SAFER
IT TEARS FAMILIES APART AND WORSENS THEIR STRUGGLE
TAKING AWAY VITAL SUPPORT FROM THOSE WHO NEED IT

"Sa Loob ng Paglaban" is a Psychological Learning Project that is a visually-engaging submission to the Psychology 101 course in the University of the Philippines. This project reflects the author's strong dedication to pursuing a better reform for Philippine Education, not just for themselves but for society as a whole.
